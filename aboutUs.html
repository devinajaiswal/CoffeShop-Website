PERFECT COFFEE MOMENTS & WELCOMING ATMOSPHERE

The birth of the Aroma Cafe can be traced back to 1999 when the first Coffeeshop opened its doors to its guests on a historical September day in United States. Characterized by a very special and unimitable atmosphere, the Aroma Cafe is a new, unique interpretation of the ever-popular traditional coffee house culuture, which was included in the UNESCO list of, intangible cultural heritage’’ in 2011.
